                                    Day 04 – HR Analytics Dataset (Multinational Company)

. Dataset Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rohitgrewal/hr-data-mnc

. Dataset Description:
  This dataset contains Human Resource (HR) information of employees from a multinational company, including department, job role, salary, experience, performance, and employment status.

. Type of Data:

  Structured (Tabular Data)

. ML Concept:
  Machine Learning / Data Analytics – analyzing employee data and predicting attrition patterns.

. Problem Statement:
  Can we analyze employee data to understand workforce trends and predict employee attrition (resignation or termination)?

. Input (Features):

    - Department
    - Job title
    - Salary
    - Years of experience
    - Performance rating
    - Work mode (On-site / Remote / Hybrid)
    - Employment status
    - Location

. Prediction / Output:

  - Employee attrition status 
  - Workforce insights 
  - Employee Status (Attrition Prediction)
  - Salary Prediction.

.  ML Problem Type:

  - Classification (Attrition) 
  - Regression (Salary)

. What We Learned:

  - Some departments have higher attrition rates.
  - Salary and experience influence employee retention.
  - Performance rating is related to salary growth.
  - Work mode affects employee satisfaction and retention.

. Real-Life Use:

  - Used by HR teams and companies to reduce employee attrition
  - Improve hiring strategies
  - Plan salaries 
  - Enhance employee satisfaction and productivity
