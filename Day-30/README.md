                                            🛍️ Day 30 – Mall Customer Segmentation Dataset

✨ Dataset Source

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/hosseinbadrnezhad/mall-customer-segmentation-dataset

---

📌 Dataset Description

This dataset contains information about mall customers.  
It includes customer age, gender, annual income, and spending behavior.

The dataset helps analyze how different customers spend money in a mall and helps businesses understand customer groups.

---

📊 Type of Data

Structured data (retail customer dataset)

---

🤖 ML Concept

Machine Learning – Clustering

Used to group customers based on similar spending and income patterns.

---

❓ Problem Statement

Can we group mall customers into different segments based on their income and spending behavior?

---

📥 Input

- Gender  
- Age  
- Annual Income (k$)  
- Spending Score (1–100)

---

📤 Output

- Customer segments or clusters

---

🧠 What We Learned

- Customers with high income may have different spending habits  
- Young customers often have higher spending scores  
- Income and spending behavior help identify customer groups  
- Businesses can target customers based on these segments

---

🔗 Correlation Insight

- Income ↔ spending behavior  
- Age ↔ spending pattern  
- Gender ↔ shopping trends
